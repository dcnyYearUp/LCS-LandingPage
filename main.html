LCS is a learning community under Year Up NY/NJ that dedicates itself to reach a level of success that will bring those apart of it a sense of fulfillment and accomplishment. We at LCS bring our best selves every single day and work to accomplish our goals, both in the short term as well as the larger grand scheme of things. Under the guidance of our program manager, Serena Tillman, as well as our coaches and the Year Up staff, LCS prides itself on accountability and the support structures set in place that allows us to keep ourselves in check. With our grit, our pride, and our sense of perserverance, LCS will one day move on to create lasting satisfying careers.
